Why NAFN?

At the National Animal Foster Network, we believe no one should have to choose between their well-being and their beloved companion. Many individuals facing medical crises, mental health challenges, addiction recovery, or domestic violence are forced to give up their pets just to receive the care they need. This is a heartbreaking choice no one should ever face.
