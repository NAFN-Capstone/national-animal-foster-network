Welcome

We're excited to share that Paws N Claws for the Cause is evolving into the National Animal Foster Network (N.A.F.N). Our mission extends beyond Colorado—reaching individuals across the nation facing crisis who need temporary care for their companion animals.

Partnering with talented computer science students at Colorado universities, we are developing a mobile app launching in 2025 to connect individuals in crisis with vetted foster caregivers. Together, we aim to reduce shelter overcrowding and euthanasia rates nationwide.
