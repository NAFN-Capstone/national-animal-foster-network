Our Story

For many individuals facing crises, the inability to secure care for their pets becomes a significant barrier to seeking essential services, including medical treatment, mental health care, substance abuse rehabilitation, and escape from domestic violence.

The National Animal Foster Network was founded to bridge this gap. Through emergency pet fostering, financial assistance, and partnerships with shelters and healthcare providers, we provide a comprehensive, sustainable solution to keep pets and people together during times of crisis.
